The Problem We Solve

The Publishing Industry Was Not Designed to Serve Authors. Legavora Was.

Most authors enter the publishing world with a finished manuscript and a clear vision. What they find instead is a system that forces them to hire separately for every single step. An editor here. A cover designer there. A marketing consultant somewhere else. A literary agent who may or may not respond.

Each vendor delivers their piece and moves on. Nobody watches the whole picture. Nobody owns the outcome. And when the launch falls flat or the book never finds its audience, every vendor points in a different direction.

That is the gap Legavora was built to fill. We are not a marketplace of freelancers. We are not a directory. We are a boutique literary agency and production house where every service an author needs lives under one name, one process, and one point of accountability. When you work with Legavora, you are not managing a team. You have a partner who manages it for you.

The Founder

Built by Someone Who Spent Nine Years Watching the System Fail Authors.

I didn't set out to build a literary agency. I started in the trenches of the freelance world, taking on every project I could find and learning the publishing industry one book at a time. Over nine years, that work became my real education.

I watched talented authors struggle through a fragmented process: hiring an editor here, a designer there, and a marketer somewhere else, hoping it would all magically come together. Usually, it didn't. I saw writers let down, sometimes quietly overcharged, but more often left with a stack of disconnected files and no one to tell them how to make it all work.

Nobody was watching the whole picture. I built Legavora to fill that gap. Every project runs through me directly. I bring in a hand-picked team of specialists to execute the vision, but I remain the single point of accountability.

Oluwaseun Sunday Olatunji
Founder & CEO, Legavora

Our Process

A Clear Process. No Guesswork. No Surprises.

Every engagement follows a structured, documented process. Here is exactly what working with Legavora looks like, from first inquiry to final delivery.

01

Project Inquiry

Everything begins with your inquiry. You submit your project details through our inquiry form or reach us directly at hello@legavora.com. Tell us where you are in your publishing journey, what services you are looking for, and what your goals are. There is no obligation at this stage.

02

Scope Review and Discovery

We review your inquiry and respond via email with any clarifying questions. This is where we build a complete understanding of your project, your goals, and what a successful outcome looks like for you specifically. All communication is documented.

03

Formal Proposal

We issue a comprehensive written proposal outlining services recommended, the full scope of work, the timeline, the terms of engagement, and all relevant details. Nothing moves forward without a proposal in writing that you have reviewed and accepted.

04

Contract and Project Activation

The contract formalizes everything in the proposal. Once signed and the initial terms are confirmed, your project is officially active. The contract covers scope, timeline, intellectual property, revision terms, and all conditions of the engagement.

05

Execution

The work happens. You receive regular updates via email. Every milestone, every deliverable, and every decision is communicated clearly and in writing. Vetted specialists are brought in where needed, under the founder's direct oversight.

06

Review, Delivery, and Ongoing Support

Deliverables are submitted for your review. Feedback is addressed within agreed revision terms. Final deliverables are transferred with full documentation. For ongoing engagements, we remain your active partner. For project-based work, we remain reachable.

Our Boundaries

What Legavora Does Not Offer.

We only take ownership of work we can deliver directly, from start to finish, with full accountability. If we cannot own it, we do not sell it.

Ghostwriting

Ghostwriting requires assuming the author's voice and producing work under their name. We develop, refine, and represent your voice. We will not substitute our own for it.

Media and Press Outreach

Securing press coverage depends on the editorial decisions of journalists and producers whose responses we cannot control or guarantee. We do not sell promises we cannot keep.

Library and Bookstore Distribution

Physical distribution through libraries and bookstores is controlled by buyers and institutional systems whose decisions are entirely outside our influence.

FAQ

Questions Serious Authors Ask Before Working With Legavora.

What kinds of authors does Legavora work with?

Legavora works with authors across fiction and nonfiction who are serious about their publishing careers. We work with first-time authors navigating the process for the first time, experienced authors seeking a higher standard of support, and published authors who need help with marketing, representation, or both. The common thread is the seriousness of intent and the quality of commitment to the work.

Do I need a finished manuscript to work with Legavora?

Not necessarily. Some authors come to us with completed manuscripts ready for editorial work. Others come earlier in their process. Where you are determines which services are relevant and in what sequence. The best approach is to submit an inquiry describing where you are and what you need.

How does Legavora handle communication?

All communication is conducted exclusively through email. This applies from the first inquiry through to the final delivery. We do not conduct business over calls, chat platforms, or informal channels. Every conversation is documented, professional, and traceable. Our central email is hello@legavora.com.

How many authors does Legavora work with at one time?

We maintain a deliberately small client roster. The number is always limited to what allows every active author to receive genuine, sustained attention. When we reach capacity, we communicate that directly and discuss expected timelines for future availability.

Does Legavora guarantee specific commercial outcomes?

No. We do not guarantee sales figures, bestseller status, or specific commercial metrics. What we guarantee is the quality of our work, the professionalism of our process, and our full accountability for every service we deliver. Any agency that promises specific commercial results is making promises it has no basis for keeping.

Can I hire Legavora for a single service?

Yes. You can engage Legavora for a single service or for a combination of services across our five pillars. Our model is built around the author's actual needs. During the proposal stage, we develop a scope of work based on what your specific project requires.

Does Legavora work with self-publishing authors?

Yes. We serve authors who want to self-publish with professional quality, authors pursuing traditional publishing, and authors who want to keep both options open. Our services support all three paths and our representation pillar specifically covers traditional publishing pursuit.

What does Legavora not offer?

We do not offer ghostwriting, media and press outreach, or library and bookstore distribution. Each exclusion reflects the same principle: we only offer services we can own, execute, and be held fully accountable for from beginning to end.

How do I get started?

Submit an inquiry through the form below or send an email directly to hello@legavora.com. Describe your book, where you are in your publishing journey, and what you are looking for. We review every inquiry personally and respond via email.
