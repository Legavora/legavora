Our Boundaries

Boundaries Define the Standard

We do not offer ghostwriting

An author's voice is their intellectual signature. It belongs to them alone. Every book Legavora represents is written by the author whose name it carries.

We do not offer press or media outreach

Earned media depends on editorial decisions outside any agency's control. Legavora does not make promises it cannot keep.

We do not offer bookstore or library distribution

These channels require negotiating with buyers whose decisions are governed by criteria outside our direct control.

A company that promises everything delivers nothing with excellence. Legavora delivers what it promises because it only promises what it can own entirely.

Our Services

A Complete Infrastructure for Every Stage of Author Success

Every engagement is tailored. To understand what working with Legavora looks like for your project, get in touch.

01

Editorial and Production

Where every professionally published book begins.

Developmental Editing

The most comprehensive editorial intervention. Examines narrative architecture, character development, pacing, and readability. Provides actionable guidance to bring the manuscript to its full potential.

Copy Editing

Line by line review addressing grammar, syntax, punctuation, sentence structure, and stylistic consistency throughout the manuscript.

Proofreading

Final review catching every remaining error and formatting inconsistency before publication. The last line of quality control.

Manuscript Formatting

Professional formatting for both print and digital. Print follows typesetting standards. Ebook ensures correct rendering across all devices.

02

Design and Media

Legavora ensures your visual assessment communicates authority and professionalism.

Cover Design and Mockups

Genre literate, visually compelling covers engineered to perform in both thumbnail and full display formats. Professional 3D mockups produced.

Interior Book Layout

Typography, spacing, margins, and print specifications executed to professional publishing standards.

Author Branding and Logo

Professional logo, color system, typographic identity, and brand guide for consistent presence across all platforms.

Author Website Design

Clean, authoritative digital presence as your professional headquarters independent of any third party platform.

Book Trailer Production

Cinematic introduction to the book's world, tone, and narrative promise. Built for video platforms and advertising.

03

Publishing Infrastructure

The technical architecture determining whether a book is discoverable or invisible.

Amazon KDP Setup

Complete configuration including format selection, territorial rights, pricing optimization, and technical setup for print and digital editions.

IngramSpark Setup

Professional print on demand infrastructure and expanded distribution extending availability beyond Amazon.

ISBN and Metadata Registration

Strategic metadata construction optimized for both discoverability and conversion across all platforms.

Amazon Listing Optimization

Keyword research, category selection, conversion copywriting, and backend configuration transforming your product page into a sales instrument.

04

Marketing and Representation

Publication is the starting line. The difference between success and failure is strategy and execution.

Book Launch Strategy

Structured pre launch, launch window, and post launch strategy designed for each title individually with measurable targets.

Email Marketing Automation

Direct, owned communication channel. Automated sequences, engagement frameworks, and promotional campaigns.

Social Media Content Strategy

Platform specific approach building credible and engaged presence where the right readers discover new authors.

Paid Advertising Management

Data informed campaigns with audience targeting, creative development, and continuous optimization.

Query Letter and Book Proposal Writing

Professional documents positioning every manuscript in the strongest competitive position within the submission process.

Publisher Representation

Active manuscript submissions, industry relationship management, and professional advocacy as your literary representative.

Contract Negotiation Support

Detailed contract review, term analysis, and negotiation support protecting your rights, royalties, and creative freedom.
